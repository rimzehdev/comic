Comics Bot Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2, 2026

Information We Collect

Depending on which features are used, Comics Bot may store:

  • Discord User IDs
  • Discord Server IDs
  • Discord Channel IDs
  • Support ticket information
  • Recruitment application responses
  • Suggestions submitted through the bot
  • Region selections used for scheduling

How Information Is Used

The information collected is used solely to provide the bot's functionality, including support, recruitment, moderation, scheduling and community management.

Data Sharing

Comics Bot does not sell or share personal information with third parties except where required by law.

Data Retention

Information may be retained only as long as necessary for the operation of the bot and its features.

Security

Reasonable steps are taken to protect stored information; however, no system can guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights

If you have questions regarding your data, please contact the Comics server administrators.

Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Continued use of Comics Bot indicates acceptance of any revisions.

Contact

For privacy-related enquiries, please contact the Comics server administrators.
